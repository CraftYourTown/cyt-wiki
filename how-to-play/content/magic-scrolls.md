# Magic Scrolls

Magic Scrolls are consumable items that permanently enchant your gear with powerful stat bonuses. Right-click a scroll onto any compatible piece of equipment to apply it. Each attribute has a cap - once reached, no further scrolls of that type can be applied to that item.

## How It Works

1. Hold the item you want to enchant in a slot
2. Pick up the scroll with your cursor
3. Right-click the scroll onto the item
4. The attribute is applied instantly and permanently

## How To Obtain

Attribute scrolls are obtainable through [Tower](towers.md) Crates

{% hint style="warning" %}
Scrolls cannot be applied inside chest or anvil inventories. Use your player inventory.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Each attribute has a per-item limit. Once an item reaches its limit for a given attribute, that scroll type can no longer be applied to it.
{% endhint %}
