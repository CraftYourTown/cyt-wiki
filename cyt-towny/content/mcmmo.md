### **What is mcMMO?**

One of the features of CYT is mcMMO, which is a plugin that gives you extra abilities for doing different skills. For a better understanding of the plugin and all its features, we have made a GUI that explains each skill and how increasing your level will give you extra abilities within that skill. By clicking on each skill in the GUI, you will get a bunch of information regarding your ability within that skill, and all the features of each skill and at what level you obtain it is also explained.
